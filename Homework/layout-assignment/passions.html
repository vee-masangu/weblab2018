Vee had dogs since her young age. She loves being surrounded by dogs. She developed the love for dogs because of her dad. At the age of 10-year-old, Vee had 10 dogs at her home. At the age of 12, Vee had her own dog that she called Pookie.

Since her family trip to Thailand, Vee's love for elephant has grown. She fell in love of their big ears, their kindness, their intelligence, and their size. Vee noticed their protectiveness to one another and also humans.

At the age of 14-year-old, Vee wanted to become a top model like her fashion icon Naomi Campbell. Unfortunately, there is no modeling agency in the DR of Congo. She then decided to own her modeling agency in her country.

Vee started riding at the age of 6-year-old. At 12, she bought her first horse named Los Angeles Times. She competed with him in the regionals. At 14, Vee bought her second horse named Connemara Volkenstein. She competed with him in the m10. Horse riding became her passion since the age of 6.
