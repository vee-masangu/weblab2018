Santorini
Santorini is the most popular island in Greece. It may be the most popular island in the world.
Located in the Aegean Sea, in the Cyclades islands, Santorini island was the site of one of the largest
volcanic eruptions in the world and what remains today is a submerged volcano and a caldera.
Dreamy beaches with crystalline water and fine white sand, geological, archaeological and cultural
landmarks steeped in ancient history and luxurious accommodation. This island knows how to
deliver the best holiday experiences to those that visit them.
